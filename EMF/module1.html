Vector Calculus Review

Vectors can be defined in three-dimensional space as:

$$ \mathbf{A} = A_x \hat{i} + A_y \hat{j} + A_z \hat{k} $$

Key operations include dot product and cross product:

$$ \mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{B} = A_x B_x + A_y B_y + A_z B_z $$ $$ \mathbf{A} \times \mathbf{B} = (A_y B_z - A_z B_y) \hat{i} + (A_z B_x - A_x B_z) \hat{j} + (A_x B_y - A_y B_x) \hat{k} $$
